How to Use BiteBalance

Log-in to the website on the home page if you want to store your calories by day

Go to the "Calendar" section to use BiteBalance. Start by searching up a description of your food.

You can type in a as many food as you would like, to describe your meal or snack.

You can put a measurement for each food item as well, such as one cup or one tablespoon.

If you would like to log your meal, select a date and click "Add to Calendar"

To view your logged foods, click on an entry on the calendar.

To remove a food item, click "Remove" next to the item you wish to remove.
