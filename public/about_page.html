About Us

Our Group

Who are we?

This is a group made of Angie Zhang, Ashhal Wasti, Emily Wright, Esther Muran, and Matt Leavitt We are Information Science students at the University of Maryland, and this project was created for our web development class, INST377 with Professor Anmol Dash.

Our Mission

Why did we create BiteBalance?

Nutrition is a difficult goal for many users, and we hoped to provide a simple tool that can make that journey a little easier. We created BiteBalance to allow users to search foods, track nutrition, and measure their caloric intake one day at a time.
